4 Player Chess (4pc)

Four-player chess is a family of chess variants played with four people. The game features a special board typically made of a standard 8x8 square, with 3 rows of 8 cells each extending from each side,and requires two sets of differently colored pieces. The rules are similar to, but not the same as,regular chess.
